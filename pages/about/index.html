About me

As a first-year master’s student at Maastricht University, Netherlands, I study Data Science for Decision Making. My field of interest includes text mining and NLP techniques, thus in my current master research project, I focus on an NLP task to transfer the styles of texts with a team of five amazing fellow students.

Before my studies, I worked as a data analyst at shopping24 in Hamburg for several years. There I gained experiences with building a company-wide data lake, making this data available in the form of insightful analyses and Tableau dashboards for all teams, and setting up apache airflow.

I’m interested in making new contacts for inspiring discussions and for exciting projects to work on. Also an internship is coming up starting summer/autumn 2021 at an European company.
